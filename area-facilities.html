The business visitor or the exhibitor can schedule a direct meeting with the key person that he needs from all the exhibitor list/registered business visitor. The attendees must present at the main exhibition counter to register their attendance. So, it can reflect on system to avoid the no show.

Due to time constraints the B2B meetings are scheduled for 15-30 minutes (vary from exhibition to another). This means that the meeting holder have to be articulate (know their business and how they can fit into the supply chain of the buyer) and demonstrate professionalism by bringing relevant marketing material (including business cards) and by being on time for their appointment.

The meeting space will contain all the facilities and all what you need for the business meetings. It contains: coffee corner for beverages, Reverse Counting clock to notify the attendees when the meeting starts and ends, Tables, sofa chairs, Tv, notes papers, printers, pens, electricity plugs, quiet atmosphere. The space will be managed by reception attendant to welcome the attendees at the waiting area and escort those who’s their time in and out. The meeting area can be customized according to your marketing needs, you can add marketing materials and branded walls, posters or any other items.
